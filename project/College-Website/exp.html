About The Institute
Indian Institute of Information Technology, Vadodara(IIIT-V) is set up by the Ministry of Human Resource Development(MHRD), Government of India under Public Private Partnership(PPP) model. The Institute was estalished in The year 2013. DA-IICT Gandhinagar has been entrusted the responsibilty of mentoring IIIT vadodara.
